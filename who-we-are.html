OUR MODEL

Equipment & Technology

We deploy world-class OEM equipment into Zimbabwe's mining sector, partnering with suppliers who have no existing footprint here, giving mines access to technology they couldn't previously source locally.

Safety & Support

Gas monitoring, cap lamps and specialised PPE, backed by full local support and training, so underground workers are protected by world-class detection systems and operations run without downtime.

Training & Maintenance

Operator training, commissioning and scheduled maintenance keep every solution running. Our local presence means faster response times than any import agent when equipment goes down.
