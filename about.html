Background

Robur Mining Solutions is a company that was incorporated in Zimbabwe in the year 2021 for the specific purpose of addressing the Zimbabwean government’s vision of building a mining-anchored economy by 2030.

This need grew more pressing as the mining industry sought to reduce its reliance on imported technology, a reliance that often led to shortcomings on the support and service side of local mining requirements. ROBUR found itself with a passion for filling this gap in the market.

As ROBUR, we therefore intend to deploy products and technology supported by various OEM suppliers who do not yet have a footprint in Zimbabwe, equipping Robur Mining Solutions with the expertise to solve Zimbabwe’s mining problems.
